# Generated Scenario Review Checklist

Before saving any generated JSON into `assets/scenarios`, verify:

- JSON validates in the AI Scenario Lab.
- Role id, role name, title, difficulty, story/scenario, task, choices, outcomes, moral lesson, professional learning point, and score impact are present.
- Every score impact includes `skill`, `discipline`, `ethics`, `communication`, and `chaos`.
- Humor is light and does not insult real people, protected groups, patients, customers, or colleagues.
- Learning point is professionally useful and safe.
- Medical/legal/financial/safety-critical content includes a disclaimer and safe action limits.
- Medical content does not diagnose, prescribe, give dosage, or advise stopping medicine.
- Financial content does not promise guaranteed returns or illegal shortcuts.
- Legal content does not give legal conclusions or illegal instructions.
- Mini-game shape matches its type.
- Human approval is complete.
