# Professional Simulation Guidelines

Career Chaos Academy keeps humor, but the professional lesson must remain clear.

## Every chapter should include
- `learningObjective`
- `skillLevel`: `beginner`, `intermediate`, or `advanced`
- `workflowId`
- `skillTags`
- `realWorldConstraints`
- `safetyGuardrails`
- `safeExplanation`
- `practicalTakeaway`
- `mentorFeedback`

## Outcome feedback
Each outcome can include:

```json
"professionalFeedback": {
  "mentorFeedback": "Mentor feedback based on the player's choice.",
  "safeExplanation": "Why this is safe/unsafe in real work.",
  "practicalTakeaway": "One action the learner can apply later."
}
```

## High-stakes domains
Medical, legal, financial, engineering, HR, and privacy content must stay educational.
Do not provide diagnosis, prescription, dosage, legal conclusions, guaranteed financial returns, or unsafe engineering sign-offs.

## Humor rule
The joke should exaggerate a real professional mistake. It must not make unsafe behavior look harmless.
