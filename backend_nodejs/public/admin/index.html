Phase 14A/14B Cinematic Asset Fields

Optional cinematic scenes are shown before mini-games and choices. Use AssetRegistry keys like bg_office_morning and char_developer_worried. Add characterId such as developer_player, senior_dev_mentor, or panic_pm_pressure to let the Flutter character engine choose emotion expressions. Direct asset paths and future remote URLs are supported as fallback.

Available sample keys:
Backgrounds: bg_office_morning, bg_production_war_room, bg_clinic_waiting_room, bg_doctor_consult_room
Characters: char_developer_worried, char_senior_serious, char_manager_panic, char_doctor_calm, char_patient_worried, char_nurse_serious

Phase 21 Audio Fields

Use audio keys from the manifest. Audio is optional and must never block gameplay. Scene audio supports BGM/SFX; dialogue audio supports SFX, future voice clips, and subtitles.

Sample audio keys:
BGM: bgm_office_light, bgm_clinic_calm, bgm_cinematic_tension
SFX: notification_ping, choice_select, success_stinger, failure_stinger, badge_unlock, comedy_bonk
Voice placeholder: voice_placeholder
